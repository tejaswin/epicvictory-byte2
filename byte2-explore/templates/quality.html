Is the data complete?

While the data does acccount for most zip codes in Pittsburgh, it is not complete.
The zip codes which had a null value, or did not have any people living in that area raised questions regarding the correctness of the data, and was hence cleaned out.
This raises the concern that our data might not show the true picture of how people in the higher money bracket are actually preferring to live.
Further, the data has been cleaned and trimmed using Wrangler to only include Zip codes inside Pittsburgh, to narrow our focus. (15200-15300)

Is the data coherent?

The data is coherent, since the incoherent data has been removed with the use of wrangler. The data that was not coherent, had string values where numeric was expected,
and that did not add up has been deleted for accuracy. This data is coherent.

Is the data correct?

The data is correct. I can verify that by cross checking the pin codes and understanding the population density of the city of Pittsburgh, and it checks out.
The data that was not correct has been removed with the use of Wrangler.
